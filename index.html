Our Story

Health Care BD was born from a simple observation: millions of people in Bangladesh struggle to access specialist doctors. Long travel times, overcrowded hospitals, and high costs prevent families from getting the care they need.

We set out to change that. By connecting patients directly with top-tier medical professionals via secure video consultations, we have eliminated the barriers of distance and inconvenience. Whether you are in Dhaka or a remote village in Sylhet, you deserve the same quality of care.

Today, our platform serves patients across all 64 districts of Bangladesh, with a growing network of specialist doctors spanning Cardiology, Pediatrics, Mental Health, General Medicine, and more.
